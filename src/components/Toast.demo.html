🎉 Toast Notification System Demo

Toast notifications provide temporary, non-intrusive feedback to users. They appear at the bottom center of the screen, auto-dismiss after 3 seconds, and can be manually dismissed by clicking.

✨ Features

  • Auto-dismiss: Toasts automatically disappear after 3 seconds
  • Manual dismiss: Click on a toast to dismiss it immediately
  • Smooth animations: Slide in from bottom with fade effect
  • Multiple toasts: Stack multiple toasts vertically
  • Success/Error variants: Green for success, red for errors
  • Accessibility: Respects prefers-reduced-motion setting
  • Responsive: Works on all screen sizes
